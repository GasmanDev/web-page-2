FIGHT FOR YOUR LIFE

PVP: Challenge yourself with intense, exhilarating PvP fighting with the greatest soldier in the open world. Gear up and use teamwork to send the enemy fleeing from the battlefield! Only the most skilful fighters will leave the field victorious!

PVE: In TOWL, zombie hunting isn't just a task; it's an opportunity for rewards. Players who engage in zombie hunts have the chance to earn currency instead of just equipment. Arm yourself strategically: The undead menace roams the world, and only the bravest survivors can triumph over them!

PLAYER-DRIVEN ECONOMY

DISCOVER, GATHER, HUNT: To erect the greatest empire and smith the most deadly weapons requires resources, and plenty of them. Search far and wide to find the very rarest, most prized materials. But be on your guard - your enemies will be looking too!

CRAFT YOUR OWN ARMOR AND WEAPONS: A player forges their own destiny! Be it a mighty set of armor, a modern gun or a set of sturdy tools - all items are crafted by you in our player-driven economy!

TRADE YOUR GOODS AT LOCAL MARKETPLACES: Have more resources and items than you know what to do with? Or are you looking for a particular kind of blade? Take yourself to the nearest marketplace – plenty of traders await you there, and you’re sure to find what you’re looking for!

A DANGEROUS WORLD

The Zombie: Zombies are a constant threat, relentless and cunning, always ready to attack survivors. Players must be vigilant and prepared to face them at any moment. Survival depends on quick reflexes, sharpshooting skills, and effective teamwork against these formidable foes.

The Bandit: Bandits are the ruthless scavengers of the wasteland, thriving in the chaos of the post-apocalyptic world. Armed with cunning tactics and a disregard for morality, they prey on unsuspecting survivors, raiding settlements and ambushing travelers for their resources. With no allegiance but to themselves, bandits are a constant threat to anyone daring to venture into their territory. Survivors must remain vigilant and prepared to defend themselves against these dangerous adversaries if they hope to survive in the unforgiving world of TOWL.

REAL ESTATE

Build a house, then set it up in style! Comfy beds, tables laden with food, hunting trophies, chests to store your valuables... Grow your collection of furniture and decorations to make your house a home.
